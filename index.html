Case Study: Caring Contacts Postcard Program

Challenge: Reduce suicide risk among discharged patients through improved follow-up communication.

Process:

  • Designed and implemented a follow-up postcard program
  • Led multiple focus groups with patients to gather feedback on language and imagery
  • Iterated postcard content based on patient input, trauma-informed care principles, and readability standards
  • Collaborated with stakeholders to operationalize postcard delivery in discharge workflows

Results: Reduced suicide-related readmissions by 15%, increased patient satisfaction, and received positive qualitative feedback from recipients.
